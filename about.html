About FastFinder

Nearby discovery without the extra scrolling.

FastFinder is a location-based discovery app built to help people find useful nearby places faster. Whether you need a cafe, restaurant, ATM, parking option, pharmacy, shop, or another everyday place, FastFinder is designed to reduce the time between “I need something nearby” and “I know where to go.”

What FastFinder does

A practical app for everyday nearby decisions.

Many people use maps several times a week, but the experience can become slow when you only need a quick answer. You may need to compare distance, opening status, rating, category, and directions before deciding where to go. FastFinder is built around that exact moment.

The app organizes common nearby needs into clear categories, so you can start from what you need instead of typing a long search every time. It is especially useful when you are walking, driving, traveling, or simply trying to make a faster local decision.

FastFinder does not try to replace full map apps. Instead, it helps you begin the discovery process faster, compare useful details, and then move toward navigation when you are ready.

  • Use case: finding useful nearby places quickly, from cafes and food to ATMs and parking.
  • Main platform: iOS app, with a lightweight web search experience on the website.
  • Location access: used to show nearby options when the user allows location permission.
  • Goal: reduce clutter, reduce searching time, and help users act faster.

Privacy and location

Location is used for nearby results, not for unnecessary profile data.

FastFinder is designed around location-based results. When you allow location access, the app can show places around your current area. If you do not allow location access, some discovery features may be less accurate or unavailable.

The app does not require users to create a social profile, upload photos, share contacts, or provide personal identity information in order to browse nearby places. The website also includes a Privacy Policy explaining the main data practices in a simple way.

The goal is to keep the experience focused: choose a category, review nearby options, and continue with navigation or more details if needed.

  • No social feed: FastFinder is a utility app, not a social network.
  • No account-first flow: nearby discovery does not require a public profile.
  • Clear purpose: location is used to improve nearby search relevance.
  • Legal pages: privacy and terms are available from every page footer.
