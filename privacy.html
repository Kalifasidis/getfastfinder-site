Privacy Policy – FastFinder

FastFinder respects your privacy.

The app collects precise location data only to provide its core functionality, specifically to help users discover nearby places such as cafes, food, ATMs, parking, and other useful locations.

Location Data

  • Used only while the app is in use
  • Not linked to the user’s identity
  • Not stored on external servers

Data Collection

FastFinder does not collect personal data such as:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Contacts
  • Photos or media
  • Payment information

Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may collect and process data such as device identifiers, approximate location, and usage data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) will be asked for consent before personalized ads are shown.
