Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

FastFinder respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how FastFinder handles location data, advertising, consent choices, and related information when you use the FastFinder app or website.

1. Location Data

FastFinder uses precise location data only to provide its core functionality: helping users discover nearby places such as cafes, food spots, ATMs, parking, pharmacies, shops, and other useful locations.

  • Location is used only while the app is in use.
  • Location is used to show nearby results and distance-based suggestions.
  • Location is not linked to your identity by FastFinder.
  • FastFinder does not store your precise location on external FastFinder servers.

2. Personal Data FastFinder Does Not Collect

FastFinder does not directly collect personal information such as:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Contacts
  • Photos or media
  • Payment information
  • User accounts or passwords

3. Place Data and Third-Party Services

FastFinder may use third-party services to display place-related information, such as names, addresses, ratings, opening hours, photos, contact details, navigation links, and other useful place information.

This information may come from third-party providers and may change over time. Users should verify important details directly with the place or provider before relying on them.

4. Advertising

The FastFinder app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. The FastFinder website may also display ads in the future through advertising services such as Google AdSense.

Advertising providers may collect or process data such as device identifiers, approximate location, usage data, consent status, and ad interaction data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and comply with applicable laws.

Learn more about Google’s privacy practices here: Google Privacy Policy .

5. Consent and Privacy Choices

Users in regions where consent is required, including the European Economic Area (EEA), may be asked to make advertising consent choices before personalized ads are shown.

If privacy options are required for your region, FastFinder provides access to privacy/ad choices inside the app settings. You can use those options to review or change your consent choices where available.

6. Website Usage

The FastFinder website is used to provide information about the app, legal pages, download links, and future web-based search features. Website advertising may be added in the future.

If website ads or analytics are added, they may involve third-party services that process technical data such as browser/device information, IP-derived approximate location, page views, and ad interaction data.

7. Data Security

FastFinder is designed to minimize direct collection of personal data. However, no app, website, or internet-based service can be guaranteed to be completely secure at all times.

8. Children’s Privacy

FastFinder is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through FastFinder, contact us using the information below.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be available on this page.
